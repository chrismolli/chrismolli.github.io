Christian Mollière

Space Nerd, AI Enthusiast, Software Engineer. · Email me!

Just finished obtaining a M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Stuttgart. Particular focus on numerical modelling, machine learning and software engineering. Currently designing & implementing system software for robotics at Franka Emika in Munich.

Completion of semester courses on non-linear optimisation & neural networks. Hands-on experience with modern machine learning frameworks. Application of bayesian-, generative and discriminative modelling in various projects. Examples include uncertainty quantification for image segmentation in earth observation and time-series regression for wind forecasting.

Hands-on experience in developing embedded firmware for the multiMIND payload at Thales Alenia Space and the robotic ROACH2 research rover. Successful deployment of image classifiers for manufacturing quality control, design of database APIs in Javascript and Python for domain-based system modelling and mechanical integration testing of solar array assemblies.
